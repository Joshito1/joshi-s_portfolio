About Us

Rucker's is a quiet, family-friendly restaurant with a professional waitstaff. Rucker's was founded in 1975 by John and Mary Rucker, who wanted to share their love of Southern cuisine with their community. They started with a small diner on Main Street, serving homemade dishes like fried chicken, cornbread and peach cobbler. Over the years, they expanded their menu and their space, but they never lost their passion for quality food and service. Today, Rucker's is a popular spot for locals and visitors alike, who enjoy the cozy atmosphere and the delicious meals.
